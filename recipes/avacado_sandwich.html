Avocado Egg Sandwich

Ingredients [For 2 sandwiches]

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 4 slices of bread
  • One Avocado

Steps

  1. Toast the bread in a toaster or on a pan using the oil
  2. Empty the avocado in a small bowl and mash it with a fork
  3. After toasting the bread fry the eggs seperately (or scramble them together and then cut it into half after)
  4. Spread the avocado paste onto the toasted bread and add the eggs in between
  5. Enjoy!
